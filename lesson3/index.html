Weather Site Plan

Site Purpose

Weather in Liberia is a weather website that is designed to be the place to find the information you need to go about your day with weather and not to have to worry about your plans for the week or the weekend. You can find all the information you need in your community, for weather, activities nearby, and locations with images to help show what it looks like during this seasons. The website is user-friendly, and anyone can access this site to their personal benefits.

weatherinliberia.org which have different prices per year by different domain provider.
