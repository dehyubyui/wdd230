Target Audience

The target audience of this site are those members of the community and board members including those seeking to get information about the community and how the weather condition and business environment look like.

Persona 1: Lroy Cassell

Leroy runs a small construction company in downtown Ocala. he works with a lot of people in the community and lots of engineering company in the Ocala area. While running those kind of company in a large city does have benefits, there are drawbacks. There is more competition, much more. New engineer in Urban areas often offer extended hours including Saturdays just to be competitive. The cost of living is expensive, as is practice overhead. As a young man , Leroy doesn't feel pressure to participate in plans as long as they know their competition participates. With our group we can help his small company stay strong and help him to grow and give back.

Persona 2: Dehyu Sinyan

Dehyu is quite know in the community for helping with finances and being a numbers guru in the local area. But when it comes to knowing most of our neighbours, and running into them frequently, can create special opportunities for advisors who live and work in small communities. We help people like Dehyu find out about the special ways he can connect with his neighbourhood clients. He can find the people he want to help and grow his business to help more of the community members.

Scenario 1

Leroy needs to see if his company will be able to open a new office in the area and if he is able to have students as well with this new building in the community.

Scenario 2

Dehyu and his team need to see if they can have clients from out of state to come and open a firm in the local area to help their business and the businesses in the surrounding area.

Scenario 3

Leroy wants to travel to train in other states and allow those in the community to help send youth to train abroad to help the community get the best service available.

Scenario 4

Dehyu wants to know if his firm can allow business not just for small but big and bigger companies can come into the local community to allow growth and oppurtunities.

Scenario 5

Leroy wants to know if the new building will help the community or make things worse for the people, as they want something else or a construction company closer to them in the local area.
