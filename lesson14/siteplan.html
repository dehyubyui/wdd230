Chamber of Commerce Plan

Site Purpose

The Ocala Community Group Executives (OCGE) works to improve economic prosperity with our intent to strive in many focused areas of business large and small, business growth and business construction. We can and will be the leading voice of business for your business. Ocala is always moving forward is our charge and it reflects our desire to be a catalyst for the business community. By working together with our partners and community investors, we will continue to improve our quality of life and build a strong base for economic growth in our community.
